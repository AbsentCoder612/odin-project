Let's Cook Steak!

The secret to cooking the perfect steak is time.

We'll start cooking as soon as we gather these ingredients

  • Steak
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Now that we've gathered the ingredients, Lets cook!

    1. Rub Salt And Pepper on steak, covering all sides
    2. Heat pan with Butter and garlic inside
    3. Cook steak for 2 minutes on each side, till darkened
    4. Bast steak with butter for 3 minutes while off heat
