Pasta

Let's cook some pasta!

Pasta is an all time favorite and super easy to make.

Let's gather the ingredients.

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Lets add all the ingredients together

  1. Cook meat in a pan. Then add sauce and cheese.
  2. Bring noodles to boil in seperate pot for 7-10 minutes.
  3. Drain noodle water.
  4. Mix meat, sauce and cheese into noodle pot.
