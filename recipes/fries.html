Fries

Let's cook some French Fries!

French fries go with almost anything!

We won't need alot of ingredients for this recipe.

  • Idaho potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil

Let's start cooking!

  1. Cut potatoes into desired fry shape
  2. Boil potatoes for 10-15 minutes
  3. Place potatoes evenly on baking sheet
  4. Spread oil and salt onto potatoes
  5. Bake fries for 20-30 minutes at 375 degrees
